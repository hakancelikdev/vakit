App Features

Prayer Times

Learn prayer times based on your location. Fajr, Dhuhr, Asr, Maghrib and Isha times.

Qibla Direction

Find the Kaaba direction by holding your phone horizontally. Learn the correct direction with the qibla compass.

Notifications

Get notifications at prayer times. Choose which prayers you want notifications for.

Hijri Calendar

View current Hijri date. Track both Gregorian and Hijri dates together.

Location Detection

Find your location with GPS. View prayer times for different cities.

Dark Mode

Dark mode for night use. Eye-friendly interface.

Works Offline

View prayer times even without internet connection. Use anywhere.
