Home Screen - Prayer Times

View current prayer times, remaining time and Hijri date at a glance. Learn prayer times based on your location. Fajr, Dhuhr, Asr, Maghrib and Isha times automatically calculated.

Qibla Compass

Find the Kaaba direction precisely by holding your phone horizontally. Learn the correct direction with GPS technology and qibla compass.

Notification Settings

Get notifications at prayer times. Choose from 11 different notification sounds: Traditional Adhan (Muhammad Al Damradash), system sounds and custom tones.

Hijri Calendar

View current Hijri date and Islamic holidays. Tap any date to see prayer times in a popup. Includes Regaib, Miraj, Berat and other important days.

Widget Support

Home screen small, medium and large widgets. Dynamic backgrounds and real-time countdown to quickly view prayer times on your home screen.

Location Settings

Find your location with GPS or search manually. 13 different calculation methods (Turkey, Umm al-Qura, Dubai, etc.) and timezone support for accurate times.

Premium Features

Live Activities (Dynamic Island), advanced widgets, ad-free experience and enhanced notification customization for advanced features.

Dark Mode

Dark mode for night use with eye-friendly interface. View prayer times even without internet connection. Use anywhere.
