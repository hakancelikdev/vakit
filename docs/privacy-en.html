Vakit – Privacy Policy

Vakit respects your privacy. We collect only the data needed to calculate prayer times, determine Qibla direction, and send local reminders. For premium users, no personal data ever leaves your device. For free users, limited data may be collected to improve the advertising experience.

Last updated: 2025

1. Information We Collect

The Vakit app collects only the minimum data necessary to provide basic functionality. No personal data leaves your device.

Data Type Purpose Processing Retention
Approximate and precise location Calculate daily prayer times and qibla direction Device only Deleted after use
Device orientation (compass) Real-time qibla compass Device only Not stored
Device motion (accelerometer / gyroscope) Compass smoothing and stability Device only Not stored
Notification settings and reminders Send local prayer notifications UserDefaults / Notification Center Until disabled or app deleted
App settings Remember your preferences UserDefaults Until reset or app deleted
Advertising Data Show personalized ads for free users Google AdMob (third-party) Limited time for ad optimization

Important: We do not collect your name, email, photos, microphone, camera, contacts, or other personal data.

2. How We Use Your Data

The data we collect is used only for the following purposes:

  • Prayer Times Calculation – Location is processed locally on device.
  • Qibla Direction – Location + direction combined on device to show Kaaba direction.
  • Prayer Notifications – If enabled, we schedule local notifications for upcoming prayers.
  • User Preferences – Stored locally so you don't have to reset every time.

Assurance: For premium users, no analytics, advertising frameworks, or remote servers are contacted.

3. Data Sharing & Third Parties

For premium users, we do not share, sell, or transmit your data to anyone. For free users, limited data sharing with Google AdMob may occur to improve the advertising experience.

4. Security

All data stays on your iPhone and is protected by iOS sandbox and encryption.

5. Your Choices & Controls

  • Revoke Location, Motion, or Notifications at any time in iOS Settings → Vakit.
  • Disable ad tracking in iOS Settings → Privacy & Security → App Tracking Transparency.
  • Purchase Premium to completely disable ads and data collection.
  • Delete the app to remove all stored data.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy to reflect new features or legal changes. You will be notified in-app for important updates.
